About Me

While my home is the DFW, I often visit my brother in beautiful, Southern California. My journey into tech started with a little Disney movie called TRON! Fascinated with how the movie's vernacular translated into the design lead me to a career in architecture. While at my architecture job, I became the "I.T. Guy" troubleshooting many user issues on the spot and soon realized my real talents may lie elsewhere. After taking a UX Design course, I was hungry to dive deeper into all things tech! Opening the door to front-end development with Skillcrsuh created a world possibilities to utilize my talents in architecture in a new and ever growing field.

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work and life. Now that I've made the switch to front-end development, I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to not only design a website, but also write clean and elegant code to make the design fully functional and responsive for all devices.
